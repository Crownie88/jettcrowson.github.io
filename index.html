The Grown-Up Course for Kids of All Ages!

Go Putt! Miniature Golf is located in The Entertainment District at Lone Tree near the intersection of C-470 and Yosemite, adjacent to the United Artists Meadows 12 Theater.

Go Putt! Miniature Golf has two unique 18-hole layouts. Each course has a waterfall, streams and challenging holes that are fun for everyone.
